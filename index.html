Mindset & accuracy reminder

This tool is here to support your fitness journey — not to judge it.

  • All values are in imperial units (lbs, inches).
  • In the future, photos may be analyzed by AI to estimate body measurements. Even then, results are estimates and may not be fully accurate.
  • Use this app to track trends over time, not to obsess over single numbers.
  • Tape-measure checks every so often are a good way to validate any AI-based values.
